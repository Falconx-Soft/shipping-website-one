Provisions Supply

Ship Provision Primorsk is a reliable supplier of provisions for ships operating in the Primorsk region. The company provides a wide range of high-quality provisions, including bakery products such as bread and rolls, as well as canned meats, dairy products, and minced meat.

For ships that require fresh food items, Ship Provision Primorsk also offers a variety of fresh produce such as fish, vegetables, and fruits. The company ensures that these items are of the highest quality and meet the required standards

In addition to food items, the company also supplies a range of beverages to ships. This includes coffee, 100% fruit juice, soft drinks, beer, wine, whiskey, rum, vodka, cognac, milk, and cocoa. The company understands the importance of providing a variety of beverages to cater to the diverse needs and preferences of the crew.

To ensure that the food items supplied are of the highest quality, Ship Provision Primorsk sources its provisions from reputable suppliers. The company also ensures that its provisions are delivered on time and in the right quantities, to meet the needs of the crew.

Furthermore, the company also supplies a range of herbs and spices, flour, sugar, and salt to ships. This ensures that the crew has all the necessary ingredients to prepare meals that are not only tasty but also nutritious.
