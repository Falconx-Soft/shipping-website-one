About Us
Ship Provision Primorsk IS OUR SPECIALTY

Working hard has made our workforce proud

At Ship Provision Primorsk, we understand the unique demands and challenges faced by ship owners, operators, and crew members. Our comprehensive range of products and services is designed to meet these specific needs, ensuring that your vessels are well-stocked and equipped for their journeys at sea.

We pride ourselves on our extensive network of trusted suppliers and manufacturers, enabling us to source the highest quality provisions at competitive prices. Our vast product portfolio includes fresh and frozen food, beverages, cleaning supplies, galley equipment, medical supplies, and much more. Whether you need provisions for a short voyage or a long-term contract, we can accommodate your requirements with efficiency and professionalism.

Our experienced team is dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and prompt delivery. We understand the importance of timely provisioning, and we work closely with our clients to ensure that their orders are processed swiftly and accurately. Our logistics experts coordinate the efficient transportation of provisions to ports worldwide, making sure that your vessels receive their supplies on time and in excellent condition.

Ship Provision Primorsk also places great emphasis on quality control and compliance with international standards. We carefully vet our suppliers and products, adhering to strict quality assurance procedures to guarantee that our provisions meet the highest industry standards. We understand the importance of food safety and hygiene on board, and we take every measure to ensure that the products we deliver are safe, fresh, and of superior quality.

In addition to our provisioning services, we offer customized solutions tailored to the unique requirements of each client. Our team of experts is available to provide professional advice and assistance, helping you optimize your provisioning process and manage your inventory efficiently. We strive to build long-term partnerships with our clients, offering personalized support and reliable service every step of the way.

Ship Provision Primorsk is committed to sustainability and environmental responsibility. We actively seek eco-friendly alternatives, such as biodegradable packaging and sustainable sourcing, to minimize our ecological footprint. We believe in the preservation of the world's oceans and strive to contribute to a greener maritime industry.

Thank you for considering Ship Provision Primorsk as your trusted partner for ship provisions. We look forward to serving your needs and providing you with exceptional service, quality products, and timely deliveries.
